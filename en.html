Dharma Time

A calendar of spiritual and astronomical events: Buddhist holidays, Hindu dates, Christian calendars, Moon phases, eclipses, meteor showers, and other celestial phenomena.

Support the project View features
🌕 Astronomy Lunar days, Moon phases, eclipses, solstices, and equinoxes.
🪷 Buddhist calendars Reminders for practice days and major holidays in different traditions.
🌠 Vaishnava calendars Indian holidays, Ekadashi, Sankranti, Purnima, Amavasya, and more.

What Dharma Time can do

The project brings together calendar systems that usually have to be searched for across different sources.

🪷

Buddhist calendars

Tibetan tradition, China, Japan, Sri Lanka, Myanmar, Thailand, and other modules.

🕉

Hindu calendars

Ekadashi, Panchanga, Vaishnava holidays, North and South India.

✝

Christian holidays

Orthodox Old and New Style calendars, as well as the Catholic calendar.

🌕

Moon

Moon phases, lunar days, moonrise and moonset, sign changes, and more.

🌠

Astronomy

Eclipses, meteor showers, comets, planetary events, Earth perihelion and aphelion.

📍

Local calculations

Many events are calculated based on the user’s city and time zone.
